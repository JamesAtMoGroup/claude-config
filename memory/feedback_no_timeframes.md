---
name: no fixed timeframes or instructor sections in course pages
description: Do not mention "三週後", specific week counts, or include instructor bio sections in course lecture pages
type: feedback
---

Never include the following in n8ncourse lecture pages:
- Any mention of "三週後", "三週內", "三週", or any fixed timeframe for completing the course
- An "關於講師" tab or section with instructor bio/credentials

**Why:** The user does not want students to feel constrained by a fixed pace ("三週"), and does not want instructor bio content shown on course pages.

**How to apply:** When building or editing lecture pages from spec files that include "三週後你能做什麼" tabs, "關於講師" sections, or "三週" references in headlines/subtitles — remove them. Replace week references with neutral labels (前期/中期/後期) or remove the timeline entirely.
